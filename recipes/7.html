Refreshing Watermelon Mint Salad

Beat the heat with this Refreshing Watermelon Mint Salad. Juicy watermelon cubes paired with crisp cucumber, feta cheese, and a hint of mint, creating a burst of summer flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups watermelon, cubed
  • 1 cucumber, peeled and diced
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp balsamic glaze
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine watermelon cubes, diced cucumber, and crumbled feta cheese.
  2. Sprinkle chopped fresh mint over the ingredients.
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze over the salad.
  4. Gently toss the ingredients to ensure an even coating of the dressing.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving to enhance flavors.
  7. Serve the Watermelon Mint Salad in a festive bowl and enjoy the perfect blend of sweetness and freshness!
