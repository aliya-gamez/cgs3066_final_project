Spicy Thai Basil Chicken

Experience the bold and aromatic flavors of Spicy Thai Basil Chicken. Tender chicken stir-fried with Thai basil, chili, and garlic, creating a mouthwatering dish thats quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups fresh Thai basil leaves
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 red chili peppers, sliced
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Steamed jasmine rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a wok or large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sliced red chili peppers, stir-frying for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add thinly sliced chicken thighs and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, and sugar. Pour the sauce over the chicken, stirring to coat evenly.
  5. Add fresh Thai basil leaves to the wok, tossing until wilted and combined with the chicken.
  6. Serve the Spicy Thai Basil Chicken over steamed jasmine rice and savor the enticing flavors of Thai cuisine!
