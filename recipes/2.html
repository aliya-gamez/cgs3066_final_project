Savory Mushroom Risotto

Indulge in the creamy goodness of this classic Mushroom Risotto. Arborio rice cooked to perfection, enveloped in a rich mushroom broth, and finished with Parmesan for a truly comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth, warmed
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  2. Add Arborio rice and cook, stirring, until lightly toasted.
  3. Pour in the white wine and cook until mostly evaporated.
  4. Begin adding warm broth, one ladle at a time, stirring frequently. Allow the liquid to absorb before adding more. Continue until rice is creamy and cooked to al dente.
  5. In a separate pan, sauté mushrooms in butter until golden brown. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Fold the sautéed mushrooms, Parmesan cheese, and additional butter into the risotto. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan and a sprinkle of black pepper.
