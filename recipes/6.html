Vegetarian Quinoa Buddha Bowl

Fuel your body with the goodness of a Vegetarian Quinoa Buddha Bowl. Nutrient-packed quinoa, vibrant vegetables, and a zesty tahini dressing come together for a wholesome and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup chickpeas, cooked
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, assemble the cooked quinoa as the base of the Buddha Bowl.
  2. Arrange cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell peppers, chickpeas, red onion, feta cheese, and Kalamata olives on top of the quinoa.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper to create the dressing.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the Buddha Bowl, ensuring even coverage.
  5. Toss the ingredients gently to combine and coat them in the dressing.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy a colorful and nutritious Vegetarian Quinoa Buddha Bowl!
