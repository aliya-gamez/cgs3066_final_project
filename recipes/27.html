Decadent Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Indulge in guilt-free luxury with this Decadent Chocolate Avocado Mousse. Creamy avocados blended with rich cocoa and sweetened with maple syrup, creating a velvety, chocolatey dessert thats as nourishing as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Fresh berries and mint leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine ripe avocados, cocoa powder, almond milk, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed for an even consistency.
  3. Divide the chocolate avocado mousse into serving glasses or bowls.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours to enhance the mousses texture and flavor.
  5. Garnish with fresh berries and mint leaves before serving.
  6. Savor the velvety richness of this Decadent Chocolate Avocado Mousse without compromising on health!
